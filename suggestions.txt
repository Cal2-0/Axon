# AXON v4.0: Enterprise Digital Forensics & Incident Response (DFIR) Roadmap
**Target Deployment: Law Enforcement, Intelligence Agencies, and National Crypto Forensic Labs**

To transition AXON from a high-tier analysis tool into a "Cellebrite-grade" institutional forensic platform, it must satisfy the rigorous demands of Lab Directors, Legal Counsel, and Agency Commanders. The platform must move beyond mere "scanning" and provide end-to-end Case Lifecycle Management, Cryptographic Evidence Security, and Deep Attribution.

The following 15 pillars represent the roadmap to secure a true Enterprise/Government deployment.

---

## PILLAR I: Chain of Custody & Institutional Case Management
*To be admissible in a court of law, evidence must be immutable, logged, and structured.*

### 1. Persistent Case Workspaces
AXON must shift to a project-based architecture. Analysts create "Case #2026-ETH-041", where dozens of scanned wallets, contracts, and OSINT reports are grouped into a single, unified workspace that persists across sessions.

### 2. Cryptographic Hashing & Chain of Custody
Every time AXON completes a scan or trace, the system generates a SHA-256 hash of the JSON/PDF evidence snapshot alongside an exact timestamp. This proves the data was pulled at that specific moment and has not been tampered with.

### 3. Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) & Audit Trails
A Lab Commander must be able to audit their own team. AXON requires strict RBAC (Admin, Lead Investigator, Analyst). Every search query, button click, and export must be logged in a tamper-proof internal audit trail to prevent insider tipping or evidence mishandling.

### 4. Dynamic Cloud Logs (Supabase Integration)
Transition from ephemeral local JSON logs to a highly secure, globally accessible Supabase backend. This allows multiple forensic labs (e.g., FBI field offices) to share real-time threat data dynamically.

---

## PILLAR II: Advanced Tracing & Graph Analytics
*Finding the needles in the haystack automatically.*

### 5. Automated "Follow-The-Money" Tracing Engine
Manual hopping is dead. The engine must accept parameters: *"Trace all outbound funds > 1 ETH up to 10 hops, stopping only if funds hit an Exchange or Mixer."* AXON automatically crawls the blockchain and generates the exact flow path.

### 6. The "Entity Evidence Board" (Multiplayer Graphing)
A Maltego-style, drag-and-drop interactive canvas. Analysts can push 50 different wallets to the board, and AXON’s engine will automatically draw the hidden connecting lines between them to expose the "Mastermind" wallet. Needs real-time collaboration (Figma for forensics).

### 7. Cross-Chain & Bridge Analytics
Hackers rarely stay on Ethereum. AXON must track assets as they jump across bridges (e.g., Thorchain, Wormhole) to Tron, Bitcoin, or privacy chains like Monero, linking the wrapped assets on the other side.

### 8. Mempool & Time-Series Activity Scrubbing
Investigators need to see *when* a wallet is active to deduce geographic timezones. Add a Gantt-style timeline scrubber to visualize "dormancy gaps" and "transaction bursts". Furthermore, add Mempool forensics to analyze pending transactions and MEV interactions.

---

## PILLAR III: De-Anonymization & Deep OSINT
*Connecting on-chain hexadecimal strings to real-world identities.*

### 9. Advanced Dark Web & Telegram Scraping
Expand OSINT capabilities to actively scrape ransomware negotiation chats, Darknet Market vendor profiles, and Telegram Drainer groups. If a wallet was pasted in a Russian hacker forum in 2023, AXON must surface that link.

### 10. Automated Subpoena & Freezing Order Generation
If AXON's tracing engine detects that stolen funds landed in Binance or Coinbase, it should auto-generate a PDF Legal Subpoena or Tether Freeze Request. The template will be pre-filled with the exchange's legal contact, deposit addresses, and tx hashes—ready for a judge's signature.

### 11. IP Telemetry & Off-Chain Correlation
Integrate with proprietary RPC nodes to capture the IP addresses broadcasting the transactions. Correlating an IP address to a wallet address is the holy grail of physical attribution.

### 12. AI Dynamic Learning & Self-Healing Intel
Static DBs go stale. When an investigator flags a new bridging exploit pattern, AXON’s AI must dynamically extract that behavioral fingerprint and auto-update the `axon_intel.db` corpus. The system learns and applies this new intelligence to all future scans.

---

## PILLAR IV: Reporting & Remediation
*Delivering the final product to the commander and the courtroom.*

### 13. Comprehensive Court-Ready Dossiers
A Lab Director requires a polished product. One-click generation of a 50-page PDF containing: Executive Summaries, MITRE ATT&CK mappings, High-Res Flow Graphs, and raw transactional appendices.

### 14. Fiat Off-Ramp Monitoring
Tracking the exact moment crypto is converted to fiat currency. AXON should integrate with banking APIs (where legally permitted) or flag the exact exchange deposit that indicates a cash-out event, notifying field agents.

### 15. Proactive "Watch & Alert" Cron Jobs
Investigations are reactive. Allow analysts to place highly suspicious, dormant wallets on a "Watchlist". If the wallet breaks a 3-year dormancy and moves funds, AXON fires a webhook (Slack/Email) to the intel team immediately.
